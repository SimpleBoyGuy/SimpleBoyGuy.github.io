About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Data Science at CUHK-Shenzhen. My recent work focuses on reinforcement learning for combinatorial optimization and scheduling, especially flexible job shop scheduling problems under safety, scalability, and structure-awareness considerations.

Before starting my doctoral study, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from CUHK-Shenzhen with first-class honors. I have also worked on smart transportation, crowdsensing, and sensing-driven intelligent systems through research projects at SRIBD and AIRS.

Research Interests

I work on reinforcement learning and optimization for complex decision-making systems, with current interests in flexible job shop scheduling, safe and structure-aware learning, multi-agent coordination, and intelligent transportation applications.

  • Reinforcement learning for combinatorial optimization
  • Flexible job shop scheduling and operations research
  • Safe, scalable, and structure-aware learning
  • Multi-agent decision-making systems
  • Intelligent transportation and sensing-driven applications
